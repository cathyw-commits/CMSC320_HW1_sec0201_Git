Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: The ability to easily track version and revert changes

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science can be applied to the transportation/shipping sector; for example, to optimize shipping routes to 
minimize cost, fuel consumption, and time in transit.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines branches while preserving their history, but rebasing 'replays' commits from one branch onto 
another and rewrites history destructively.
